DWA V&V System — Internal Application Wireframes

Interactive wireframes for the DWS/CMA internal MVC application. Click any card to view the wireframe.

01 of 12

Dashboard

National overview: KPI cards, V&V progress by WMA, overdue tasks, letter tracking, and catchment status at a glance.

02 of 12

Property List & Search

Browse, filter, and search all properties by SG code, WARMS registration, WMA, owner name, or validation status.

03 of 12

Case Detail (FileMaster)

Central case view with tabbed layout: overview, water use, calculations, letters, documents, audit trail, and workflow status.

04 of 12

Workflow Progress Tracker

Control point progression through all 21 steps, guard validation, evidence checklist, and transition requests.

05 of 12

Water Use Data Entry

Field & Crop entry with SAPWAT calculation, irrigation records, dam calculations, and forestation/SFRA data.

06 of 12

ELU Determination Summary

Validation outcome: all 9 scenario categories, qualifying vs current comparison, lawful/unlawful volumes, registration status.

07 of 12

Letter Management

Generate, preview, sign, issue, and track Section 35 letters. Batch processing and response recording.

08 of 12

Letter Signing

Electronic signature capture with SignaturePad, document integrity verification, and signature request management.

09 of 12

Notifications & Inbox

In-app notification centre with unread indicators, action links, and notification preference settings.

10 of 12

Administration

User management, role assignment, organisational unit hierarchy, reference data, and public user approvals.

11 of 12

Reports

Catchment progress, letter tracking, validation summary, and user activity reports with export to PDF/Excel.

12 of 12

Audit Log

Immutable action log with advanced filtering, entity history timeline, and case-level audit trail for PAJA compliance.
